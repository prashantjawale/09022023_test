Welcome to Maynooth University

The National University of Ireland, Maynooth, commonly known as Maynooth University, is a constituent university of the National University of Ireland in Maynooth, County Kildare, Ireland. Maynooth University ranked #1 in Ireland in the Times Higher Education (THE) Best Young University Rankings

St Joseph's Square

It was begun in 1798. The north side was completed in 1809 and called New House. The south side of what was now beginning to look like a square was completed between 1822 and 1824. St. Joseph’s Square has character, despite questionable refurbishment.

Eolas Building

Located in the heart of the North Campus, Eolas is a landmark building. It is 4 storeys high with a substantial 2 storey overhang at the south facing main entrance, creating a dramatic welcome on approach. The use of strong colour in the delightful mosaic benches, in combination with the red soffit, creates a vibrant social hub at this sunny focal point of the campus.
